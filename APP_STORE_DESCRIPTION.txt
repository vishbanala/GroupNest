GroupNest - Private Spaces for Friends

Keep lists, plans, and photos all in one clean app with your closest friends.

GroupNest is the perfect app for small groups of friends who want to stay organized and connected. Whether you're planning a trip, sharing restaurant recommendations, or keeping track of group goals, GroupNest makes it easy to collaborate without the chaos of group chats.

KEY FEATURES:

🏠 Private Groups
Create invite-only spaces with custom names, icons, and themes. Each group has a unique join code, so you control who gets access. Perfect for close friends, family, or any small group that wants a private space.

📋 Shared Lists
Collaborate on lists with your group. Add items with descriptions, tags, photos, and links. Mark items as complete, see who added what, and keep everything organized. Great for:
• Restaurant recommendations
• Travel planning
• Movie watchlists
• Shopping lists
• Goal tracking
• Activity ideas

📁 Categories
Organize your lists into categories like Food, Travel, Activities, Shows/Movies, and Goals. Keep everything tidy and easy to find.

📸 Photo Feed
Share memories with your group in a beautiful photo feed. Add captions, react with emojis, and organize photos by list. It's like having a private photo album just for your group.

👍 Voting System
Let friends vote on list items to make group decisions together. Perfect for deciding where to eat, what to watch, or which activity to do next.

💬 Comments & Discussions
Keep conversations organized around lists and items. No more scrolling through endless group chat messages to find that one recommendation.

PERFECT FOR:

• Planning trips with friends
• Sharing restaurant and bar recommendations
• Tracking group fitness goals
• Organizing activities and events
• Keeping memories together
• Making group decisions
• Staying organized without the mess

PRIVACY-FOCUSED:

GroupNest is designed with privacy in mind. Your groups are private and invite-only. Only people with your join code can access your group. No public profiles, no discovery features—just you and your friends.

CLEAN & SIMPLE:

We believe in keeping things simple. GroupNest has a clean, intuitive interface that gets out of your way so you can focus on what matters: staying connected with your friends.

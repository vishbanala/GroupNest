GroupNest - Private Spaces for Friends

Keep lists, plans, and photos all in one clean app with your closest friends.

WHAT'S NEW IN THIS VERSION:
• Bug fixes and performance improvements
• Enhanced photo sharing experience
• Improved list organization
• Better group management tools

KEY FEATURES:

🏠 Private Groups
Create invite-only spaces with custom names, icons, and themes. Each group has a unique join code, so you control who gets access.

📋 Shared Lists
Collaborate on lists with your group. Add items with descriptions, tags, photos, and links. Mark items as complete, see who added what, and keep everything organized.

📁 Categories
Organize your lists into categories like Food, Travel, Activities, Shows/Movies, and Goals. Keep everything tidy and easy to find.

📸 Photo Feed
Share memories with your group in a beautiful photo feed. Add captions, react with emojis, and organize photos by list.

👍 Voting System
Let friends vote on list items to make group decisions together. Perfect for deciding where to eat, what to watch, or which activity to do next.

💬 Comments & Discussions
Keep conversations organized around lists and items. No more scrolling through endless group chat messages.

PERFECT FOR:
• Planning trips with friends

• Tracking group goals
• Organizing activities
• Keeping memories together

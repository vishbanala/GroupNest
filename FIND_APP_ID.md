# How to Find Your App Store Connect App ID

The error says: "This step can be skipped by providing ascAppId in the submit profile."

We need to find your App ID and add it to eas.json.

## Step 1: Find Your App ID

1. Go to [appstoreconnect.apple.com](https://appstoreconnect.apple.com)
2. Click **"My Apps"**
3. Click on **"GroupNest"** to open it
4. Look at the URL in your browser:
   - It will look like: `https://appstoreconnect.apple.com/apps/1234567890/appstore`
   - The number `1234567890` is your App ID (ascAppId)

## Step 2: Share the App ID

Once you have the number, share it with me and I'll update your eas.json file.

The App ID is typically 8-10 digits long.

APP STORE CONNECT - BUNDLE ID INFORMATION

When creating your app in App Store Connect, you'll see a field for "Bundle ID". Here's what to know:

BUNDLE ID: com.vishbanala.GroupNest

This must match EXACTLY with:
- Your app.json file (ios.bundleIdentifier)
- Your build configuration
- Your provisioning profile

DESCRIPTION FOR APP STORE CONNECT:

The bundle ID is a unique identifier for your app. You don't need to write a description for it in App Store Connect - you just select it from a dropdown if you've already registered it, or create a new one.

If you need to register a new Bundle ID:
1. Go to Apple Developer Portal → Certificates, Identifiers & Profiles
2. Click "Identifiers" → "+"
3. Select "App IDs"
4. Enter: com.vishbanala.GroupNest
5. Select capabilities (see below)
6. Register it

CAPABILITIES TO ENABLE (in Apple Developer Portal):

When registering your Bundle ID, enable these capabilities:

✅ App Groups (if you plan to share data between apps)
✅ Associated Domains (for deep linking)
✅ Push Notifications (if you want to add them later)
✅ Sign in with Apple (optional, for future use)

Note: Photo Library and Camera access are handled through Info.plist permissions (already in your app.json), not Bundle ID capabilities.

CURRENT APP CAPABILITIES (Already Configured):

In your app.json, you have:
- NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription: "GroupNest needs access to your photos to let you share them with your groups."
- NSCameraUsageDescription: "GroupNest needs access to your camera to let you take photos to share with your groups."

These are Info.plist permissions, not Bundle ID capabilities. They're already set up correctly.
